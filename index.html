FAQ

What is GHacks all about and why should you join?

Where is it?

The Hackathon will be hosted in the Georgia Center, on the campus of UGA.


What does it cost?

It is free to participate and hack - you may even win prizes!


Do I need a team?

You do not have to hack in a team. If you want a team you can bring one or we will help you find one at the event.


What should I bring?

Bring your computer and any hardware you want (some hardware will also be provided but it is limited).


When is it?

We are tentative schedule to hack from March 20th to March 22nd


Is experience required?

No experience is required - You can learn while you're here. We will even have workshops to help you.


Will food be provided?

Lots of food - glorious food - will be given to you free of charge.


Will travel be reimbursed?

Your travel expenses will be reimbursed so long as you notify us when you register.
