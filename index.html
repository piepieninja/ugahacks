About

What is UGA Hacks all about and why should you join?

  • Build Your Skills

    Vetran hacker? Brand new to programming? Awesome! We welcome people from all over the spectrum. All we want you to have is the drive and desire to learn and make cool stuff. There will be seminars and workshops to get your started and broaden your knowledge! More experienced attendees can hone their programming skills by applying their technical expertise to real market issues, gain practical experience, and have a chance to be innovative with cutting edge software.

  • Become Valuable In The Jobs Market

    Technical companies love hackathons because they bubble up the smartest and most creative programmers, developers, designers, and engineers around! Recruiters will be at the event checkin you out! Attending the event will also allow you to upload your resume that we personlly will hand over to them.

  • Learn From Motivated People

    Have one to one meetings with experienced developers, engineers, and designers. Hackathons are the mecca for motivated people ready to come up with the next big thing. The atmosphere is full of excitement and collaboration.

  • Win Prizes

    Of the many companies that are sponsoring UGAHacks, almost all of will be hosting their own themed competitions. Not only do you have a chance to win prizes from these companies, but you also have a chance to network! Much money, very prestige.
